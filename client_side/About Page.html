Initial Idea


The inital idea of the SMME business is to provide Students in Busiess with a platform that will help them efficiently keep track of their business without having to get involved in the technical part of the business that involves calculation and more.

Main Purpose


SMME Finance aims to assist businesses with keeping track of their business affairs such as:


  • Trading Stock
  • Customer Base
  • Profits
  • Product Sales
  • Document of Financial Standing

The Goal


The goal is to build or improve young enterprise owners' efficiency in busines and ways of formulating strategies, in order to best maximise the profits of their business.
